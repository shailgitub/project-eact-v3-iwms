Super Meteor 650 FAQs

  • What is the price of Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 Motorcycle?

    The Super Meteor 650 comes in 3 variants (Astral, Interstellar & Celestial)

    Astral - INR 3,63,900*
    Interstellar - INR 3,79,123*
    Celestial - INR 3,94,347*
    All prices are ex-showroom Chennai.

  • What is the fuel tank capacity of Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650?

    The fuel tank capacity of Super Meteor 650 is 15.7L, Reserve capacity is approx. 3.1L and Usable volume is 14.5L.

  • What kind of engine is there on Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 Motorcycle?

    Super Meteor 650 is our iconic parallel twin engine, which offers seamless gear transitions, strong low-end acceleration, and effortless throttle response. A 648cc parallel-twin engine with Max power - 47PS @7250 RPM and Max Torque 52.3Nm@5650 rpm.

  • How many colours are available in Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650?

    The Super Meteor 650 comes in 3 variants (Astral, Interstellar & Celestial), each with its own colour range, totalling to 7 standard colour options across variants.

    Super Meteor 650: Astral - Black, Blue, Green
    Super Meteor 650: Interstellar - Green, Grey
    Super Meteor 650 Tourer: Celestial - Red, Blue.

  • What is the Mileage of Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650?

    Weather and riding style can influence the mileage of your Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 motorcycle, however as per CMVR, the fuel efficiency of Super Meteor 650 is 22 kmpl.

  • What is the seat height of Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650?

    The seat height of Super Meteor 650 is 740 mm.

  • How much does Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 weight?

    The kerb weight of Super Meteor 650 is 241 kg.

Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA

  • Whats the collaboration Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S all about?

    Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 brings you SENA 50Sa world-class connected device that comes equipped with an array of features to make each ride a smoother experience. From exceptional audio and Mesh Intercom technology with a bluetooth range of 2KM, with an option to extend it a further *8KM; it keeps you connected with your fellow riders no matter how disconnected you are from the rest of the world.

  • Where can I purchase it?

    You can purchase the Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S through at store.royalenfield.com

  • Whats the price of the Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S?

    Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S comes at an exclusive price of 35,990/-

  • Which are the features of Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S?

    Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 brings you SENA 50Sa world-class connected device that comes equipped with an array of features like
    SENAs Mesh Intercom technology
    SOUND BY Harman Kardon and FM radio capabilities
    Multi-Channel Communication (upto 9 channels) and Private Conversations
    Intercom Range 2 km / 1.2 mi (extendable upto *8km/4.6 mi)
    Advanced Noise Control
    Google / Siri Voice Command
    Bluetooth 5.0 platform compatibility

  • What is the range of the bluetooth connection?

    Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S lets you to effortlessly toggle between tuning into your favorite playlist, talking with your fellow riders across a 2KM bluetooth range, which can also be extended to *8KM.

  • Do Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S have voice assist?

    The SENA 50S multi-language voice command feature lets you take charge of all its features in eight different languages.

  • What is the warranty time period for Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S?

    Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 x SENA 50S user-friendly design allows for effortless installation across a wide range of helmets. With minimal tools required, you can quickly enjoy the benefits of SENAs technology.
